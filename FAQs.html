Testimonials

AllerSafe is a personal dietary guide designed to help individuals with specific dietary needs navigate the complex world of food allergens and dietary restrictions. It provides features like a store locator, user reporting, community page, recipe suggestions, offline functionality, recommendation system, and store navigation.

AllerSafe helps you identify if a food product is safe to consume based on allergens, lactose intolerance, and religious dietary considerations. It also provides alternative product suggestions, recipes that align with your dietary restrictions, and a community page for discussions and suggestions.
